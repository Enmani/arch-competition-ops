# Vision

## Product

Arch Competition Ops is an AI-first procurement intelligence product for licensed architects and design teams.

It helps professional practices find, screen, and act on public design opportunities with a credible path to built work.

## Who It Serves

- Licensed architects
- Established studios
- Multidisciplinary design teams
- Practice managers and business development leads

## What It Must Be

- Official-source-first
- Procurement-first
- Evidence-traceable
- Qualification-aware
- Useful for real team decisions, not just browsing
- Bilingual by default

## What It Is Not

- Not a generic competition gallery
- Not a homeowner renovation marketplace
- Not a free-form public posting board
- Not an inspiration, awards, or media-first product
- Not a generic CRM or project-management suite

## Core Promise

Turn fragmented official notices, authority pages, and secondary discovery signals into structured, trustworthy opportunity intelligence.

The product should help teams answer:

- Is this real?
- Is this relevant?
- Are we qualified?
- Is there a credible implementation path?
- Should we bid?

## Winning Direction

We do not win by looking newer than legacy directories.

We win by being:

- faster at converting official notices into usable intelligence
- better at cross-country and multi-language normalization
- clearer about qualification fit and implementation path
- more embedded in the team's pursuit workflow after discovery

## Non-Negotiables

- Official facts outrank secondary discovery
- Missing facts stay unknown until verified
- Public surfaces consume normalized records and do not invent facts
- Professional utility matters more than vanity traffic
